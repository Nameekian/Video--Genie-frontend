Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Genie (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google, we collect your Google ID, name, email, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: We may collect logs such as the files you convert, formats used, and errors encountered.
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, and device type for security and analytics purposes.
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions, payments are processed by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, Flutterwave). We do not store credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • Differentiate between free and premium users.
  • Authenticate users securely via Google sign-in.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity.
  • Comply with legal obligations where required.

3. File Handling and Retention

Uploaded files are processed solely for conversion and download. We automatically delete uploaded files within 24 hours. We do not archive, resell, or distribute user files.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information. We may disclose information only:

  • To comply with legal or regulatory obligations.
  • To trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers).
  • If required for enforcing our Terms of Service.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Video Genie may use cookies and third-party analytics tools to monitor traffic, measure performance, and enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures (encryption, secure hosting, HTTPS) to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under this age. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. International Users

If you are accessing Video Genie outside Nigeria, please be aware that your information may be processed and stored in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of our Services indicates acceptance of updates.
